Sex differences in gproxy disaggregated by race/ethnicity and parental education (SES), with heterogeneity tests and SAT/ACT external validity.

Race × Sex Interaction

Sex differences in gproxy vary across race/ethnicity groups. Heterogeneity tests show significant variation in NLSY79 (p < 0.001) and NLSY97 (p < 0.001) but not CNLSY (p = 0.88).
